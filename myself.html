Introduction

My university graduation project was titled "Using EEG to Explore Responses to Stimulating Activities." In this project, I used EEG devices to observe emotional and brainwave changes in individuals experiencing stimulating activities. The objective was to collect and analyze whether people exhibit similar brainwave responses under the same stimuli. Additionally, I compared the EEG data with external factors such as personality traits and fear levels indicated in questionnaires to validate the accuracy of the EEG data. The ultimate goal is to apply this research to testing human-machine interfaces or assessing user concentration in virtual reality environments, aiming for more objective data analysis. I also have six months of experience in MIS, which I hope will be beneficial to my research career.
